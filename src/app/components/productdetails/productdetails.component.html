Fidelity Small Business Account (FSBA) (Starter/Micro)
₦10,000 minimum account opening /operating balance
No account maintenance fee for all debit turnover amounts as long as customer maintains the N10,000 minimum daily operating balance required to enjoy free transactions on the customer’s FSBA (Starter/Micro) account
If at the end of any day, the FSBA (Starter/Micro) account is discovered to have a credit balance below N10,000, all debit transactions on that day on the account will be charged account maintenance fee at N1/mille. All accrued charges on the account will be taken at the end of the month. However, if the FSBA (Starter/Micro) account under reference is not funded at the end of the month in which the charge has accrued, it will be carried forward and taken at the end of the following month. If the charge is still pending, it will continue to be carried forward to subsequent months until the charge is successfully taken.
No fixed monthly charges
No limit on number of withdrawals
Easy access to loans
Free business advisory services
Access to e-banking services
Fidelity Premium Business Account (FPBA) (Variant 1)
₦100,000 minimum opening/operating balance.
Maximum account-maintenance-charge-free monthly turnover –N50m
No monthly maintenance charge
Account will be charged maintenance fee at N1/ mille on the transaction responsible for the account balance going below the minimum operating balance of N100,000 (one hundred thousand naira only) and charge is applied online, real-time
Any debit turnover recorded on the account in excess of the monthly charge-free debit turnover amount of N50m will be charged account maintenance charge (AMC) at N1/mille
0.5% P.A interest on average monthly credit balance
Free initial cheque book
Access to loan facilities
Access to electronic banking services – cards & online banking
